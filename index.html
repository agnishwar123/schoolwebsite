campus Life

School Church

was built in 1877

Calcutta Boys' School has a very well designed Church used to offer prayers to Jesus Christ.

Laboratories

was built in 1914

Calcutta Boys’ School has 4 well equipped Science Laboratories, designed to conduct Practicals for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science respectively. Each laboratory provides facilities for conducting Practicals pertaining to the prescribed syllabus of CISCE for classes IX to XII. Computer Education Starts much early, from class I. The laboratories are equipped with the latest apparatus required by the Council.

School Library

was built in 1892

Calcutta Boys’ School Library is a heritage library with a collection of more than 13000 Books. Students use the library for their reference work. All Books are arranged according to Dewey Decimal classification. Boys of classes II TO IV come to this library for reading. Classes V to XII can borrow books from library. Book fair are being organised by the library thrice a year & book club twice a year. Books by various authors fined pride of place in the library, enriching our collection of every year.
